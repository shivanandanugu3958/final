THE BREAKTHROUGH DEDICATED ADVISORS

Breakthrough Manchester at SNHU's College-Bound program features dedicated advisors who are highly motivated, well-organized, and passionate undergraduate students, graduate students, or recent alums. These advisors serve Breakthrough Manchester College-Bound students towards becoming powerful self-advocates by checking in with them virtually every week, and in-person roughly every month. Students can expect personalized coaching on building essential skills, planning a high school path, searching for best-match-and-fit colleges, and completing applications
